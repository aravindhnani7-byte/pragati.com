Visionary Milestones

Direct Interaction with IISR Scientists
ICAR–IISR signed a non-exclusive license in 2017 with Mr. Pidikiti Chandrasekhar Azad of Guntur, Andhra Pradesh for the turmeric variety IISR-Pragati. The variety is high-yielding (38–52 t/ha), short duration (180 days), rich in curcumin (5.02%), and moderately resistant to root-knot nematode. It is suitable for cultivation in several Indian states and supports the supply of quality seed rhizomes to growers and entrepreneurs.
